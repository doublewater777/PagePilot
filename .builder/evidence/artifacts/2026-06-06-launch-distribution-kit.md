## Risk Notes

- Name clarity: web search shows other products using "PagePilot" for AI page builders and website builders, so English distribution should use "PagePilot eBook Reader" or "PagePilot for Apple Watch page turning" when ambiguity matters.
- Compliance wording: keep claims factual. Avoid "best", "guaranteed", "private forever", or broad health/productivity promises.
- Privacy wording: supported claim is local-first/no external server for books and reading data; OPDS downloads connect directly to third-party providers.
- Payment wording: avoid listing exact prices in social posts unless copied from current App Store pricing.

## Core Message

PagePilot is a local-first iPhone/iPad ebook reader that lets you turn pages from Apple Watch. It is for people who read with their phone or iPad on a stand: in bed, at the gym, on a train, or anywhere reaching for the screen breaks the reading flow.

## Message Spine

- Target user: iPhone/iPad readers with Apple Watch.
- Pain: reading posture is interrupted by repeated screen taps/swipes.
- New capability: turn pages by tapping Apple Watch or rotating the Digital Crown.
- Supporting features: EPUB/PDF/CBZ support, WiFi local transfer, iPhone-iPad transfer, reading progress, local reading stats, system TTS.
- Trust point: no cloud bookshelf; books and reading data stay local.
- CTA: download PagePilot on the App Store and try Watch page turning with a local book; use the official website as a secondary detail/privacy link where useful.
