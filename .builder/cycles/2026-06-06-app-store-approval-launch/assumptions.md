# Assumptions

## Riskiest Assumption

"Apple Watch page turning" is immediately understandable and desirable enough to make readers try a new ebook reader.

## Supporting Assumptions

- Reading comfort matters in specific scenarios: bed, gym, high-speed train, phone/iPad on a stand.
- Local-first privacy is a useful trust signal for ebook readers.
- Chinese platforms should use concrete scenarios rather than generic app-launch language.
- English channels should say "PagePilot eBook Reader" to avoid confusion with other PagePilot products online.

## What Would Falsify This

- Posts get polite likes but no questions, saves, clicks, or install intent.
- Users focus on generic ebook-reader features and ignore Apple Watch page turning.
- Name confusion appears in replies.

- README feature list.
- Website copy in `docs/index.html`.
- Privacy policy in `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` and `docs/privacy.html`.
