# Goal

## User Problem

Readers who already use iPhone/iPad for local ebooks still need to reach for the screen to turn pages. This is especially annoying when reading in bed, on a stand, at the gym, or on a train.

## Target User

Apple ecosystem readers with an iPhone/iPad and Apple Watch who care about local books, privacy, and a comfortable reading posture.

## Desired Behavior Change

Users try PagePilot because they recognize the "hands-free page turn" pain, import a book, open it, and turn pages from Apple Watch.

## Success Criteria

- At least 3 channels receive meaningful comments, saves, clicks, or DMs.
- At least one channel produces App Store visits or install intent.
- Messaging clarifies that PagePilot is an ebook reader, not an AI page builder.

## Non-Goals

- App Store / Google Play metadata is out of scope for this distribution pass.
- Official website rewrite is out of scope for this distribution pass.
