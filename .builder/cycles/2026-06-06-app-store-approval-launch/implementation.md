# Implementation

## Scope

Generate first-launch distribution copy for selected channels after App Store approval.

## Included

- Core message.
- Chinese platform copy for WeChat official account, Toutiao, Xiaohongshu, Jike, Shaoshupai, and V2EX.
- English copy for Product Hunt-style draft, X/Threads, LinkedIn, Reddit/Hacker News, and email/newsletter.
- Copy-ready final posting pack with titles, bodies, first comments, reply templates, screenshot order, and posting order.
- Launch checklist and risk notes.

## Excluded

- App Store / Google Play metadata.
- Website copy rewrite; use the existing website only as a secondary detail/privacy link in launch posts.
- Actual posting to public channels.
