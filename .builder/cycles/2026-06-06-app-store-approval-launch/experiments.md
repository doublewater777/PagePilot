# Experiments

## Planned Experiments

1. Post the launch copy to Jike.
2. Publish a practical article version to WeChat official account and Toutiao.
3. Publish a visual workflow version to Xiaohongshu.
4. Publish a builder/productivity angle to V2EX or Shaoshupai if the tone fits.
5. Publish an English short version to X/Threads and Product Hunt-style draft when ready.

## Measurement Plan

Track manually for 72 hours:

- comments
- saves/favorites
- DMs
- App Store clicks when available
- installs or direct user feedback
- questions about Watch page turning, local import, privacy, or Pro

## Pass Threshold

At least one channel shows clear pull: useful comments, questions, DMs, or installs, not just likes.

## Stop / Loop-back Condition

If reactions are mostly "interesting" but no action, loop back to the wedge and test narrower scenarios: bed reading, gym reading, train reading, or iPad stand reading.
