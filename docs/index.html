极速局域网 WiFi 传书

Lightning WiFi Transfer

无需任何数据线或第三方云服务。只需在同一 Wi-Fi 下，通过电脑浏览器访问手机端地址，拖拽文件即可秒速导入书籍。

Discover near-by devices via Bonjour. Effortlessly send files between your iPhone and iPad on the local network without PC relays.
